# ⚽ Mini's Kickoff — Match Tracker

A simple sideline app for grassroots kids football. Track the timer, score, substitutions and player time — then share a match report with parents in one tap.

---

## Installing on Your Phone

Mini's Kickoff works like a native app when added to your home screen. Here's how:

**iPhone (Safari only):**
1. Open the app URL in **Safari** — it won't work if added from Chrome
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen (box with an arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it **Mini's Kickoff** and tap **Add**

The app will appear on your home screen with a green icon. Tap it to open full screen with no browser bars, just like a regular app.

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app URL in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three dot menu** in the top right
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

---



Open the app in your browser and you'll see three tabs at the top — **Match**, **Squad** and **POTM**.

Before kickoff, set up your squad in the **Squad tab** and choose your half length and sub alert timing on the **Match tab**.

---

## Match Tab

This is your main screen during the game.

- Tap the team names to rename them
- Hit **▶ Start** to begin the countdown timer
- Tap **+** next to your team's score to log a goal and select who scored
- Tap **+** next to the opponent's score to add their goal instantly
- Tap **−** to remove a goal if entered by mistake
- Use **⏸ Pause** during half time
- Hit **▶ 2nd Half** to start the second half
- If the game ends early tap **🏁 End Early**

**Half length** and **Sub alert** timing can be set before kickoff using the buttons that appear below the timer.

---

## Squad Tab

Manage your players here during the match.

- **On Field** — players currently playing. Tap any player to sub them off
- **On Bench** — players waiting. Tap any player to bring them on
- Tap **✎** to edit a player's name
- Tap **🤒** to mark a player as absent — they'll be ignored for substitutions
- Tap **Restore** to bring an absent player back
- Add new players using the box at the bottom

When a substitution is suggested, the player who should come off will **flash orange 🟠** and the player who should come on will **flash blue 🔵**.

---

## Substitution Suggestions

The app automatically suggests substitutions to keep playing time fair for every player. When the gap between a field player and a bench player reaches your set threshold (default 2 minutes), an alert appears on the Match tab.

You can tap **Sub** on the alert to apply it instantly, or ignore it and make your own decision — you're always in control.

Absent players are never included in substitution suggestions.

---

## POTM Tab

At the end of the match, select your **Player of the Match** here.

- Tap a player to select them
- Optionally add a photo by tapping **📷 Add Photo**
- Tap **📋 View Match Summary** to see the full report

---

## Match Summary

The summary shows the final score, goals scored, Player of the Match and each player's time on the field.

To share with parents tap **📤 Share Match Report** — this opens your phone's share sheet so you can send it via WhatsApp, iMessage, email or any other app.

If sharing doesn't open automatically, tap the **⎘** icon to copy the report and paste it manually.

---

## Tips

- Set your squad up before the season starts — player names are saved automatically to your device
- Mark players as 🤒 absent before kickoff so subs are calculated correctly
- The app works best in **Safari on iPhone** — add it to your home screen for the full app experience
- Each device saves its own data locally — squad info won't carry over to another phone
